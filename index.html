Who We Are

BUILT TO MAKE THE IMPOSSIBLE HAPPEN

Raven Staging is a specialist event infrastructure and fabrication company delivering extraordinary physical experiences for the world's most ambitious creative projects. From intimate installations to landmark spectaculars, we build the structures that make extraordinary ideas real.

From Concept to Construction

We work alongside creatives, producers and directors from early design through to final delivery — bringing technical rigour to bold artistic visions.

Specialists in the Unique

No two projects are the same. Our workshop handles everything from bespoke steel fabrication to container conversion to full outdoor stage builds.

Bradford & Beyond

Proud infrastructure partners for Bradford 2025 UK City of Culture, and trusted by international clients including the Doha Debates — all built and delivered right here in the UK.
