Raven Staging is a specialist event infrastructure and fabrication company. The right skills and the right equipment, in service of the work. As your project evolves, so do we.

From Concept to Construction

We are experienced in working alongside creatives, producers, directors and community leaders - from early design through to final delivery — bringing technical rigour to ideas, from the fully formed to the back-of-a-napkin.

Specialists in the Unique

No two projects are the same, and neither are the people behind them. We come to every brief with an open mind, adapting our approach to serve the vision rather than the other way around.

Reliable By Nature

We understand the pressure of tight timelines and last-minute changes — and we have the experience, the team and the resources to deliver.

What We Do

OUR SERVICES

01
Staging

Our mobile trailer stage and scalable Litedeck inventory cover everything from one-off shows to major productions, backed by a broad, bespoke fabrication capability.

02
Crew Provision

From load-in to get-out, we provide skilled, ticketed and local crew for productions of any scale. Reliable, experienced and ready to work.

03
Fabrication

From the delicate to the robust, our workshop team brings any brief to life. Carpentry, metalwork, mechanics, electrics, creative development — and much more besides.

04
FOH / Projections

Purpose-built and adaptable, our projection support ranges from covered Layher towers to submersible screens — infrastructure tailored to the demands of high-impact experience.

05
Equipment Hire

The essentials that keep events running. Truss, ballast, festoon, fencing, cable ramps, gazebos — we stock the fundamentals so you don't have to source them elsewhere. One supplier, less hassle.

06
Full Event Delivery

We have delivered multiple events end-to-end — city centre festivals spanning culture, food, drink and entertainment. Documentation, logistics, infrastructure, personnel, partner liaison and marketing all handled in-house, from first brief to final close.
