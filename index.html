OUR SERVICES

01
Staging

Our mobile trailer stage and scalable Litedeck inventory cover everything from one-off shows to major productions, backed by a broad, bespoke fabrication capability.

02
Crew Provision

From load-in to get-out, we provide skilled, ticketed and local crew for productions of any scale. Reliable, experienced and ready to work.

03
Fabrication

From the delicate to the robust, our workshop team brings any brief to life. Carpentry, metalwork, mechanics, electrics — and much more besides.

04
FOH / Projections

Purpose-built and adaptable, our projection support ranges from covered Layher towers to submersible screens — infrastructure tailored to the demands of high-impact experience.

05
Equipment Hire

The essentials that keep events running. Truss, ballast, festoon, fencing, cable ramps, gazebos — we stock the fundamentals so you don't have to source them elsewhere. One supplier, less hassle.

06
Full Event Delivery

We have delivered multiple events end-to-end — city centre festivals spanning culture, food, drink and entertainment. Documentation, logistics, infrastructure, personnel, partner liaison and marketing all handled in-house, from first brief to final close.
