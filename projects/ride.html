Project

Ride — Spin Arts / BD25

2025

Back to Portfolio

Ride was a large-scale theatrical production by Spin Arts, presented as part of Bradford's UK City of Culture year at a major indoor venue. Raven Staging designed and built the core structural infrastructure for the show — a multi-level Layher scaffold platform system, erected on top of a Litedeck stage base. Across the front face of the Layher structure, we fabricated three bespoke 4m x 3m projection screens mounted on runners, allowing the creative team to slide and reposition the screens mid-performance. This system enabled a constantly shifting theatrical environment, with each screen configuration creating an entirely different visual set — from intimate domestic scenes to expansive urban landscapes. The result was a production infrastructure that gave the creative team genuine flexibility, built to the precision that a technically ambitious live show demands.
