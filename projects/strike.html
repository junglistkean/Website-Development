Project

Strike — BD25

2025

Back to Portfolio

Strike was one of Bradford 2025's most visually iconic commissions — a dramatic illumination and activation of the city's historic mill chimneys as a statement of industrial heritage and civic pride. Raven Staging provided the on-site technical and structural support to bring the installation to life, coordinating access, rigging, and power infrastructure across a challenging urban environment. The project required careful planning around listed structures and public safety, delivered under tight event deadlines.
