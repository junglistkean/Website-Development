Project

We Will Sing — Ann Hamilton / BD25

2025

Back to Portfolio

We Will Sing was an immersive participatory work by American artist Ann Hamilton, commissioned as part of Bradford's UK City of Culture programme. The installation brought together hundreds of community voices in a purpose-built acoustic environment. Raven Staging provided the structural and technical support to realise Hamilton's vision at scale — managing the fabrication and installation of the physical framework that shaped the sonic and spatial experience. A project that demanded precision, sensitivity to the artistic brief, and close collaboration with the wider production team.
